About us

Grinnell Designs is a leader in costume jewelry manufacturing and product development, with a fully operational facility situated in the heart of New York's garment district.

Since its inception in 1994, Grinnell has worked with a lot of fashion and jewelry houses, including Escada, Doncaster Collection, Bill Blass New York, Michael Kors, Isaac Mizrahi, Tommy Hilfiger, Lela Rose, Peter Som and Zac Posen, to name a few.

Armed with skilled professionals in product development, design, and production/manufacturing, Grinnell continues to supply the fashion and jewelry industries with the best quality products manufactured locally, with the option of producing large quantity/mass-market goods through its supply partners overseas.

Grinnell offers exceptional service in the following areas of production

  • Product Development
  • Modelmaking
  • Molds (master casting and/or production)
  • Jewelry/product assembly
  • Soldering
  • Metal cleaning and polishing
  • Spin casting (lead free white metal)

All techniques in assembly and jewelry manipulation, including

  • Hand knotting (pearls/beads)
  • Wire wrapping (single, full-wrap, rosary-links)
  • Gluing (including rhinestone pave')
  • Enameling and enamel color mixing
  • Bead stringing
